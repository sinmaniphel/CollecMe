# CollecMe

## Approach

### General
* "having a collection" = having ownership of a set of instances of a particular identified object
* I want to have access to all the pertinent information to a given "collectible" regardless of whether it is owned or not
* I want to describe my collections by picking collectibles I own
* I want to add some additionnal informations on the particular piece I own
* I don't believe there are such things as "classifications of collectors"

### On themes
* I want my collections to have themes
* Some of these themes may be broad and shared (eg numismacy)
* Some of these themes I may want to define for myself
* A given theme may have a finite, known, number of collectibles (eg stamps) or not (ex canes)
* A given public theme may have its own "code" such as
  * Refusing public quotations
  * Not disclaiming ownership
  * etc

### On items
* A given item may correpond to several themes
* A given item may have several characteristics
* If there are several owners, and they are willing to provide informations of there own item, I'd love to see them on the same page
* I may want to add some information regarding an item, but keep it to myself

### On item caracteristics
* A characteristic must be as broad as possible

### On ownership
* I want to declare that I own a type of item just by clicking "I own one"
* I want to declare that I would love a collectible by clicking "I'd love one"
* I want my ownership to be publicly disclosed, or not
